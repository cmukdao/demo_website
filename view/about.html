About Us

We are your neighborhood custom bakery in Pennsylvania, where every confection is a unique creation. We're dedicated to bringing your sweetest visions to life, from bespoke cakes to hand-decorated cookies, all made to order with love. Step into a world where traditional baking meets modern design, and each bite is a celebration. Come and taste the difference that personal touch and local flavor make at Say it with Sweets.

Let us help make your next event special by creating a cake, cupcakes or cookies to match your theme. We also offer our famous gourmet cupcakes for any occasion or just because you want some.. Be sure to browse our website for gifts such as cookie bouquets, chocolate-covered treats or cookie trays, certain to delight anyone.
